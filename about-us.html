Look at them, they come to this place when they know they are not pure. Customers use the forks, but they are mere trespassers. Only I, Mr. Noir, know the true power of the Mac & Cheese. I was cut in half, destroyed, but through it's Cuisinart, the Mac & Cheese called to me. It brought me here and here I was reborn. We cannot blame these creatures, they are being led by a false prophet, an impostor who knows not the secrets of the Cuisinart. Behold the Customers, come to scavenge and desecrate this sacred realm. My brothers, did I not tell of this day? Did I not prophesize this moment? Now, I will stop them. Now I am changed, reborn through the energy of the Mac & Cheese. Forever bound to the Cuisinart. Let it be known, if the Customers want true salvation, they will lay down their cutlery, and wait for the baptism of my Cuisinart. It is time. I will teach these trespassers the redemptive power of my Cuisinart. They will learn it's simple truth. The Customers are lost, and they will resist. But I, Mr. Noir, will cleanse this place of their impurity.
